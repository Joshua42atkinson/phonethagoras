About Phonethagoras

Your private System Console — right in your pocket.

A free tool built for people who are rebuilding. No accounts, no servers, no tracking. Everything stays on your device.

What Is Phonethagoras?

A self-mastery engine anyone can use — with nothing to install, sign up for, or pay.

Phonethagoras is a free, private AI self-mastery engine that runs entirely on your phone. It helps you organize your life story, build a resume, manage stamina, and prepare for your next encounter — all without sending a single byte of data to anyone.

  • No login required — open the site and start immediately
  • No server, no cloud — the AI runs directly on your device
  • No cost, ever — free at launch, free forever
  • Works offline — after your first visit, it works without internet
  • Complete privacy — all data stays on your phone, period

Built by Joshua Atkinson — disabled veteran, Purdue University Learning Design & Technology program, Systems Architect & Instructional Designer. Created because everyone deserves a free tool to organize their life.

What Does It Do?

Four specialized tools and one personal guide — each designed to meet you where you are.

🌲
The Scout (Career Explorer)

Paste your work history — military service, odd jobs, volunteer work, anything. The AI translates it into resume-ready bullet points that recruiters actually understand.

📖
The Storyteller (Story Writer)

Write about a hard time in your own words. The AI finds the strength in your experience and reframes it into professional language you can use on applications and in interviews.

⚕️
The Nurse (Wellness Guide)

Feeling overwhelmed? Start a guided breathing exercise to reset your nervous system, or talk through what's going on. Includes crisis resources (988 & 211) if you need them.

🏛️
The Professor (Job Matcher)

Paste a job posting. The AI compares it against your skills and experience, showing exactly which requirements you meet and which gaps to work on next.

💎
Zen Zuse — The Architect

Not sure where to start? Just tell Zen Zuse what's going on. Your personal AI guide will listen, ask the right questions, and point you to the tool that fits your situation.

How to Get Started

Three steps. Under two minutes. No downloads.

1

Open Phonethagoras on your phone

Visit phonethagoras.com in any browser. It works on iPhone, Android, laptops — anything with a modern browser.

2

Enter your name and a short story

Tell the app what to call you and where you're at right now. Use your real name or a nickname — whatever feels safe. This never leaves your device.

3

Pick one goal to work on

Choose something specific — "get a job in IT," "build a resume," "learn to manage stress." The AI breaks it into steps and guides you from there.

Privacy & Compliance

Phonethagoras is private by architecture, not by policy. There is no server to breach.

Regulation How We Comply
GDPR / CCPA Complete data sovereignty. All data lives on the user's device. Clearing browser data = permanent, irrecoverable erasure.
HIPAA Bypassed entirely — zero Protected Health Information (PHI) is transmitted across any network, ever.
FERPA & COPPA Student records are isolated on the local device. No data is collected, stored, or shared by any external party.
WIOA Local scripts compile client data into downloadable compliance reports — ready for your existing intake workflows.

Operational cost: $0.00 per user at infinite scale.
