Quick Reference

💬

Talk to The Architect

Open the chat and tell Zen Zuse what's going on. Type or tap the mic to talk. It'll ask the right questions and point you to the right tool.

📖

Equip The Storyteller

Go to the Storyteller. Write about a hard time in your own words. The AI extracts your strengths and turns them into professional language.

🏛️

Equip The Scout

Go to The Scout. Paste a job posting. The AI shows which skills you have and which gaps to work on next.

🌿

Equip The Nurse

HP running low? Go to The Nurse for a guided breathing exercise. It's a voluntary stamina reset — drink a potion and step back into the arena.

📤

Sync with Guild

Generate a Party Sync, then tap "Sync with Guild." Your mentor gets a summary of your stats — EXP gained, unlocked skills, and needed buffs.

🔒

Your Privacy

Everything stays on your device. No login, no server, no tracking. Clear your browser data to erase everything permanently.
