# CONTEXT.md - The Theoretical Framework

> **[CORE DIRECTIVE]**
> This document establishes the psychological and pedagogical foundation of Phonethagoras. All system logic, UX decisions, and AI interactions must align with the theories and principles outlined herein.

---

## 1. Pedagogical Foundation: Self-Determination Theory (SDT)

Phonethagoras operates strictly within the framework of Self-Determination Theory (SDT), which posits that human motivation and psychological well-being require the fulfillment of three innate psychological needs: **Autonomy, Competence, and Relatedness.**

The LitRPG (Literary Role-Playing Game) wrapper is not an aesthetic overlay; it is a structural mechanism designed to fulfill these three needs systematically.

### I. Autonomy: The Sovereignty of the Main Character
In traditional clinical or casework environments, the user is structurally disempowered. They are the "patient" or "client" subject to the interventions of a "provider." Phonethagoras reverses this power dynamic. 
- The user is the **Main Character**. 
- The AI is the **System Console**, operating entirely in service to the user's sovereign intent. 
- All data generation, retention, and sharing requires deliberate action from the user. The system possesses zero proactive authority to act upon the user's data without explicit command.

### II. Competence: The Artifact Forge
Administrative barriers—such as composing resumes, navigating Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act (WIOA) paperwork, or filing Veterans Affairs (VA) claims—are historically designed to induce friction, eroding the user's sense of competence. 
- Phonethagoras addresses this via the **Forge Mechanism**.
- By reframing bureaucratic labor as "Artifact Crafting," the system validates the raw data of the user's lived experience (their "EXP") and structurally transforms it into high-utility professional outputs. 
- Competence is achieved not by "fixing" the user, but by equipping them to translate their inherent value into institutional currency.

### III. Relatedness: The Guild Architecture
Isolation exacerbates psychological distress. However, traditional case management often relies on hierarchical, surveillance-based relationships.
- Phonethagoras replaces the casework hierarchy with **P2P Party Syncs**.
- Mentors, case workers, and peers are positioned as "Fellow Travelers" or "Guild Masters." 
- Communication is peer-to-peer, executed via agnostic protocols (e.g., WhatsApp deep-linking), ensuring that the user experiences connection as an act of voluntary alliance rather than obligatory reporting.

## 2. Pedagogy: Consent as a Game Mechanic

Consent within Phonethagoras extends beyond legal compliance; it is integrated as a core pedagogical tool. 

When the application requires access to the device's microphone for Speech-to-Text (STT) capabilities, or requires local storage permissions, the prompt is structurally reframed. 
- The user is not merely dismissing an Operating System dialogue box.
- The user is actively "Unlocking a Skill" or "Equipping a Tool."
- This process conditions the user to recognize their own agency. Every interaction with the system is an intentional choice, reinforcing internal locus of control.

---

## 3. Trauma Processing: The Raid Framework

Trauma and acute emotional distress are handled devoid of clinical pathology. 
- Crises are not classified as "symptoms" of a broken psyche; they are classified as **Raids** or **Boss Encounters**.
- If a user is overwhelmed, the system operates on the assumption that they are simply "under-leveled" or lacking sufficient "stamina" for their current environment.
- This semantic shift removes shame and blame. It allows the user to objectively analyze their circumstances and logically deploy resources (e.g., Somatic Regulation / "Stamina Regen", or escalating to professional crisis lines / "High-Level Paladins") without internalizing the struggle as a permanent character defect.
